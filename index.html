ISABELLE & YONG

ARE GETTING MARRIED Dec 30, 2017

About us

We met at the airport in Taiwan at when we were both college student in National Taiwan Normal University. Then Yong moved to US for his graduate study and Isabelle came years later to join him. We registered for marriage in Maryland at 2014 and decide to have a wedding in Malaysia.

Wedding venue

Our wedding ceremony will take place on December 30th, 2017 at the Gita Bayu in Seri Kembangan, Malaysia at 10 a.m. At the same night, we will have a reception in Port Dickson, Isabelle’s hometown. The next night, we will have a Buffett in Jorak, Muar, Yong’s hometown.
